About

For some 20 years I have been involved in Credit Risk, Credit Management, I started at HFC in 1991, while studying for my ACCA qualification, underwriting Personal loans, Car Finance and Home Improvement Loans, as well as being involved in a project which saw the launch of the Jessop Card.

I then moved to Paragon Mortgages in 1996 where I spent 7 years underwriting Buy to Let Mortgages, being involved with large broker business, and high value portfolios. Managing a small team of underwriters. During my time there I obtained my CeMap Qualification.

In 2003 I moved to Lombard, working in the Lombard Vehicle Management team, underwriting HP and Lease contracts. In 2006/7 the team became part of Lombard Credit and we then underwrote items from Cars to Cranes, Portacabins to Yatchs. I then started work for SCH Group, the largest privately owned Computer software and hardware distributor in europe, as a Credit Analyst, assessing small medium amd multinational companies for credit limits, providing reports for the FD and MD with recomendations of limits. I also liaised with Credit Insurers to obtain cover where possible.

Since moving to Doncaster I have been setting up my consultancy business, with a view to assisting small and medium sized businesses who do not have a credit risk/control department to manage their risk, and collect monies owed, freeing them up to carry on their business.

Work

My consultancy business has been set up to assist small and medium sized companies to manage risk, in terms of offering credit to their customers, by providing a risk report on the creditworthiness of those customers.

I can offer a collections facility for companies who do not have a credit risk/control department to protect credit terms offered and collect monies owed.

I have 20+ years of experience in this field and a strong analytical process to provide a high quality and efficient service to clients.

I am also studying with the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers at present with level one completed.
